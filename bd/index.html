Design Collective

Bertrand Design was founded in 2005 to deliver a way to publish research data on the Internet. The result was a new, bespoke content management system, complete with user manual, and a couple of guys who wanted more…

The founding partners, brothers Benjamin & Adam Bertrand, went their separate ways to pursue their own design disciplines. Adam's wanderlust landed him in Korea. He returned with a black belt in kum-do, a family of four, and love of whitespace in Occidental design patterns.

Ben followed his vision of bringing online publishing to the marketing departments of the Midwest, albeit a few years before it's time. He took his love of typography & mathematics online by programming brand-faithful websites. Offline, he married his college sweetheart and enjoys fatherhood, gardening and cooking.

Since 2008, Bertrand Design operates as a collective of creative professionals that matches the best available talent with our client's projects. Our individual strengths are complimentary, and the production teams are tailored to our client's needs. This is how he deliver:
Better results. By design.
