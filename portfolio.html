Dakota Prairie Outdoors

XHTML | CSS | PHP | Photoshop | Illustrator

Dakota Prairie Outdoors is a hunting and fishing information radio program that airs five days a week, Monday through Friday across North and South Dakota.

The program host, Scott Bachmeier, needed a logo design and a site built to highlight his show, allow the online listening of current and archived shows, display photos of his achievements and current happenings in the Dakotas through a blog.

The use of an authentic photo of North Dakota and wood background sets the mood for the site. The use of PHP and LongTail's JW Flash player allow for the dynamic access of the current radio program on the landing page and on the archive page. Cohesive integration with a Wordpress blog allows users to access further community content.

Highlights: PHP and MySQL Database driven content combined with the LongTail JW Flash player.
