About Adam

Adam is the lead web developer at smith&jones | idea agency in Sturbridge, MA and a part-time freelance web designer. He has plenty of experience building HTML5, CSS3, javascript standards-compliant websites. He also does extensive work in Wordpress building custom themes for his clients.

After graduating in 2004 from South Dakota State University with a BA in Graphic Design he has spent 5 years living in South Korea, teaching English as a second language and doing freelance web design. Since August 2011 Adam has helped smith&jones bring the bleeding edge of web development to their clients in New England.

When he is not working he enjoys spending time with his family, watching movies and/or learning new things.
